Dates from 10 DAYS OF FANTASTIC THAILAND

Itinerary
  • Day 1

    Day 1: Arrival in Phuket & Transfer to Krabi

    Arrive at Phuket Airport, complete immigration, and meet our representative for your transfer to Krabi. Enjoy a scenic 3-hour drive through lush tropical landscapes. Check into your hotel upon arrival and spend the rest of the day at your leisure. Overnight stay in Krabi.

  • Day 2

    Day 2: 4 Island Tour by Speed Boat

    After breakfast, embark on a thrilling speedboat tour of Krabi's stunning islands. Visit Phranang Cave Beach, Tup Island, Poda Island, and Chicken Island. Enjoy time on the beautiful beaches, take in the striking limestone cliffs, and relax by the ocean. Lunch will be provided during the tour. Adventure seekers can enjoy water sports before returning to the hotel. Overnight stay in Krabi.

  • Day 3

    Day 3: Transfer from Krabi to Phuket & Cabaret Show

    After breakfast, check out and transfer to Phuket. After checking into your hotel, unwind before an evening show at the famous Simon Cabaret, where you'll enjoy a captivating cultural performance. Overnight stay in Phuket.

  • Day 4

    Day 4: Phi Phi Island Tour

    Start your day with breakfast, then head to the Phi Phi Islands by speedboat. Explore iconic spots like Maya Bay and Monkey Beach, and admire limestone cliffs and pristine beaches. A lunch stop is included on the islands before returning to Phuket. Overnight stay in Phuket.

  • Day 5

    Day 5: Phuket City Tour & Evening at Leisure

    Following breakfast, embark on a city tour to visit popular landmarks, including Karon View Point, Wat Chalong Temple, and the Big Buddha Statue. After sightseeing, the rest of the evening is yours to relax or explore. Overnight stay in Phuket.

  • Day 6

    Day 6: Fly to Bangkok & Transfer to Pattaya | Free Evening

    After breakfast, transfer to Phuket Airport for a short flight to Bangkok, where you’ll be greeted and driven to Pattaya. Check into your Pattaya hotel and later, later evening free at Pattaya

  • Day 7

    Day 7: Coral Island Tour with Lunch

    Enjoy breakfast before a boat ride to Coral Island. Spend the day at Tawaen Beach, where you can relax or engage in activities like snorkeling and sea-walking. Return to the hotel in the evening. Overnight stay in Pattaya.

  • Day 8

    Day 8: Pattaya to Bangkok & City Tour

    After breakfast, depart for Bangkok and check into your hotel. Later, visit popular sites like the Golden Buddha Temple and Temple of the Emerald Buddha, and shop for gemstones at the Gems Gallery. Overnight stay in Bangkok

  • Day 9

    Day 9: Full Day Safari World & Marine Park

    Following breakfast, spend the day at Safari World and Marine Park. Encounter exotic animals in the open-air zoo, see dolphins and sea lions perform, and enjoy a meal at one of the park’s dining areas. Overnight stay in Bangkok.

  • Day 10

    Day 10: Departure

    After breakfast, pack your bags and transfer to Bangkok Airport for your journey home, filled with wonderful memories of Thailand.
